About Seahonor

Seahonor is a professional organization providing comprehensive corporate services including audit, tax, accounting, consulting and corporate secretarial services, mainly to foreign investment enterprises in China including Fortune 500 companies. Established in 1999, Seahonor has consistently endeavored to offer high-quality and efficient professional services to our clients. It has been recommended by Shanghai Committee of Commerce to foreign investors as Class A Consulting Company since 2006.
